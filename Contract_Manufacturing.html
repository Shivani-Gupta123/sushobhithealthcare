Contract Manufacturing

Sushobhit Health Care Pvt. Ltd. is WHO-GMP, ISO, & GLP certified company that is known for offering qualitative Pharma Products. We are engaged in offering an extensive range of products consisting of Pharmaceutical Tablets, Pharmaceutical Capsules, Pharmaceutical Syrups, Pharmaceutical Suspension, Pharmaceutical Oral Drops, Cosmetic Products, Skin Care Products , Veterinary Products and many more to name.

We are proud to be one of the most respectable PCD Pharma Franchise company in India offering the best way to promote healthy lives across the country. We manufacture quality pharma products at cost-effective prices while offering the business deal to the individuals to grow their business by connecting with us.



Experience

As a top performer among pharmaceutical contract manufacturers, we have over 25 years of expertise and experience in wide variety of formulations, and offer contract manufacturing in pharma for various dosage forms.

Quality Control

We have strong and well developed Quality Control and Quality Assurance systems with online monitoring of status of your products, in line with the requirements of corporate sector pharmaceutical companies in India

Research & Development

Sushobhit Pharmacheuticals emphasize tremendous value in research & development (R&D) of our products. R&D has always been a key aspect of our process as this helps us to meet the changing needs of our customers and stay ahead of competitors by introducing new products and improving existing ones

State of the Art Facility

We have a good facility made strictly as per Schedule M and have all necessary area segregations, air pressure differentials and systematic work environment beating various pharmaceutical contract manufacturing companies in India Simultaneously we are dynamic and have adaptability to incorporate flawlessly into supply chain for various dosage forms up to full satisfaction of our customer.
